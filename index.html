about

Joel Humphries is an academic researcher and writer working on Wurundjeri Country in Melbourne, Australia. He is a PhD candidate at RMIT University, where he examines queer community using emerging internet technologies, such as the metaverse and the blockchain. Joel’s practice interrogates the intersection of queerness and technology, culminating in his cult newsletter, Queer Computer.

His work has been published by Continuum (2025), Media International Australia (2025), Overland literary journal (2022) and Crawlspace (2023). Previously, Joel worked as the Curator of The Waiting Room Project and the Membership Coordinator of the Museum of Contemporary Art Australia (MCA).
