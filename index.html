about

Joel Humphries is a writer and researcher working on Wurundjeri Country. He recently commenced a PhD at RMIT University, where he examines queer community using emerging internet technologies, such as artificial intelligence and the blockchain. Joel’s practice interrogates the intersection of queerness and technology, culminating in his cult newsletter, Queer Computer.

Elsewhere, his work has been published by Overland literary journal (2022) and Crawlspace (2023). Previously, Joel worked on Gadigal Country as the Curator of The Waiting Room Project and the Membership Coordinator of the Museum of Contemporary Art Australia (MCA).
